Morph, Video Art, 2020

One of the first video projects that I have done. I took an image of clouds which had been colored in blues and golds, and then I made multiples of the same image but with different perspectives until all the images morphed together. The idea came to me when I was thinking about how images moved when under the influence of psychedelics.

Video Installation, Video Art, 2020

Some early work that I had done using some video editing software that wasn't Premier Pro. I got the idea from watching the music video "Someday" by Wevel, in which it show various mundane objects being spun in a circle until it turned into a morphed imaged showing various patterns. So I decided to use tiles as my images to use and made them move clockwise until the tile becomes a moving picture.
